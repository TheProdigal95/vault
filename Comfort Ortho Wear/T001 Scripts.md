## Script #1 — 60-Day Arch Support Timeline

**Persona:** Long-Shift Standing Worker
**Structure:** Progression timeline
**Duration:** ~75 seconds
**Caption style:** Bold sans-serif + milestone cards
**Close type:** Urgency/offer close
**VO voice:** Female, 35-50, steady and confident. Narrator authority — guiding through milestones, not a character.

### Concept

A progression timeline that tracks what happens to a nurse's arch support over 60 days in ComfortWear's ULTIMATE INSOLES. Single-focus: arch support only. No wide toe box, no slip-resistance in the body. The structural backbone is explicit day milestones (Day 1, 7, 14, 30, 60) that give the viewer a reason to stay. Past-perfect framing at each milestone ("your arch HAS rebuilt") creates certainty rather than hope. Nurse persona established in every hook. Hospital B-roll anchors the pain context.

|---|---|---|
| Temporal discounting milestones | Ad 10 ($2.6K, 1.91 ROAS, hook 66) | Five escalating milestones, each naming the arch mechanism at that stage |

| Hospital-specific context | Ad 1 ($28K, 1.29 ROAS) | Concrete floors, 12-hour shifts, scrubs B-roll throughout |
| Fast cuts, 2-3s per shot | Ads 1-5 (ranked format pacing) | Same rhythm across body milestones |

## Script #2 — 3-Shoe Ranked Comparison

**Persona:** Long-Shift Standing Worker
**Structure:** Ranked comparison
**Duration:** ~80 seconds
**Caption style:** Bold sans-serif with score highlights (orange/yellow)
**Close type:** Proof cascade
**VO voice:** Female, 35-50, objective and assured. Product reviewer energy — evaluating each shoe.

### Concept

A tight 3-shoe ranked comparison that tests Skechers, Hoka, and $300 podiatrist orthotics against ComfortWear. Each competitor gets one specific disqualification (foam compresses, too heavy, too expensive) before ComfortWear wins with all three mechanisms answering those failures. Structural backbone is "who wins?" competition arc. Modeled directly on the $70K ranked comparison format (Ads 1-5) but tightened from 5 competitors to 3 for pacing. Competitor names spoken in VO, logos blurred in footage.

## Script #3 — AI Nurse Greenscreen — Sneakers Are Making It Worse

**Persona:** Long-Shift Standing Worker
**Structure:** Mechanism explainer (AI animated character)
**Duration:** ~64 seconds
**Caption style:** Two-tier: sans-serif narration + bold serif keywords
**Close type:** Urgency/offer
**VO voice:** Female, 45-55, direct and experienced. Must match AI character — confident nurse explaining to a colleague.

### Concept

An AI nurse character, composited over hospital B-roll and CGI overlays, delivers a contrarian mechanism explainer: your running sneakers are actively worsening your plantar fasciitis on 12-hour shifts because they're designed for impact absorption during forward motion, not static standing weight distribution. The greenscreen format allows CGI overlays (foam compression animation, arch support cross-section, weight distribution heatmap) to appear directly behind or beside the character as she explains. The structural backbone is a promise in the hook ("here's what nobody tells you about your work shoes") that pays off in the mechanism reveal (foam compression vs. structural arch support). Single-focus on ULTIMATE INSOLES.
