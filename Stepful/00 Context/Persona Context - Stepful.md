## Client Feedback Addendum — June 2026 Audience Mix

The review-derived personas above remain useful, but client feedback adds five explicit audiences that future batches should rotate in so Stepful is not framed as only for moms/single moms.

1. **Pre-med students (18–25)** — Online and part-time format lets them follow along while taking pre-med classes. Completing the clinical externship can strengthen a med school application because it shows hands-on clinical exposure beyond job shadowing.
2. **Caregivers** — People who have cared for a family member and now want to enter healthcare, but do not have time for years of med school.
3. **Military spouses** — Strong audience because government tuition coverage can apply.
4. **Amazon Flex / Amazon employees** — Strong audience because Amazon can cover tuition.
5. **Career switchers** — People in dead-end jobs with no benefits who want a practical change into healthcare.

**Creative mix rule:** Moms/single moms are a valid Life-Circumstances segment, but they cannot dominate the batch. Balance against pre-med, caregiver, military spouse, Amazon-funded, and broader career-switcher angles.

## The Affordable Fast-Track Seeker — "I needed certification without wasting years or thousands"

**Audience share:** 22.1% of reviews. Primary persona. Precision: 78%.
**Top 3 pains:** Time cost of traditional programs, tuition unaffordability, inability to attend in-person school while working

---

### Part 1 — How Stepful Connects

This persona's core tension is real: they want healthcare credentials but can't afford 1-2 years and $10,000-30,000 at community college or a traditional school. They work a full-time job, have a schedule that won't accommodate in-person attendance, and need to learn while earning.

Stepful's 4-5 month timeline + online format + need-based grant program (most out-of-pocket students qualify) + biweekly payment plan addresses every blocker directly. The live Zoom classes (twice weekly) give structure without requiring full-time commitment. The clinical kit sent to their home lets them practice without a campus. Certification prep aligned to the NHA, PTCB, or DANB means the program has a clear finish line: pass the national exam, get the credential, enter the workforce. For this persona, Stepful's 78-86% CCMA pass rate above the national average is credibility proof that fast doesn't mean shallow.

### Part 2 — Customer Profile + VoC

Who they are: People working full-time jobs who want to enter or advance in healthcare without stopping their life. Many are already juggling work and family. They found Stepful through social media (TikTok mentioned specifically) or online searches, were initially skeptical about an online program, and ultimately chose it because of the price, speed, and flexibility combination.

## The Externship & Job-Placement Deceived — "I enrolled for the job promise — the job never came"

**Audience share:** 19.8% of reviews. Primary persona. Precision: 90%.
**Top 3 pains:** Externship placement delays/failures, unresponsive coordinators post-graduation, feeling deceived by job placement marketing

---

### Part 1 — How Stepful Connects

This persona enrolled primarily because of the job coaching, externship booking platform, and implied job guarantee. They completed the coursework and passed their exam — the things entirely in their control — and then hit a wall when it came to the parts Stepful controls: externship placement and job support. Many are lower-star reviewers whose experience was split: "the classes were great, but everything after was a disaster."

This persona tests Stepful's weakest delivery point. For the ad context, this matters because it's the second-largest persona and their experience gap creates the most damaging perception risk. The **positive** version of this persona — students who DID get placed and DID get hired — show that the system works when execution is there. Those students credit specific coaches (Crystal Rubin, Tracye Warfield, Michael Guerrero, DaVita as externship site).

### Part 2 — Customer Profile + VoC
