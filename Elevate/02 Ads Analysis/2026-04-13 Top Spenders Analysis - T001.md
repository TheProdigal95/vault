### Key Observations

1. **Every top spender is about price.** Payment plans, $0 down, $54-58/week, Affirm/Afterpay/Klarna logos. There's no education angle, no mechanism story, no ingredient breakdown. People already want GLP-1 — the bottleneck is cost.

2. **The IG Stories UI format is the most efficient template.** Three variants in the top 12, all performing well. The blueberry and green leaf backgrounds both work. "No insurance? No problem" outperforms "GLP-1 for an affordable price."

3. **Premium design is a consistent thread.** Noise texture gradients, frosted glass cards, clean typography. Even the comparison chart (Ad 12) uses this design language. It makes medical weight loss feel aspirational rather than clinical or sketchy.

4. **Voice of customer is barely present.** Only Ad 08 uses VoC, and it's the most interesting creative in the top 12 — magazine article format, real gym mirror selfie, authentic feel. This is the biggest opportunity gap.

5. **UGC video formats don't convert in this account.** Great for brand awareness but $0 purchase ROAS on multiple ads. The audience that clicks on dancing POV videos isn't the audience that buys.

6. **No Reddit-style or social post ads exist in the account.** This is a completely untested format.
