## Script #1 — 60-Day Arch Support Timeline

**Persona:** Long-Shift Standing Worker
**Structure:** Progression timeline
**Duration:** ~75 seconds
**Caption style:** Bold sans-serif + milestone cards
**Close type:** Urgency/offer close
**VO voice:** Female, 35-50, steady and confident. Narrator authority — guiding through milestones, not a character.

### Concept

A progression timeline that tracks what happens to a nurse's arch support over 60 days in ComfortWear's ULTIMATE INSOLES. Single-focus: arch support only. No wide toe box, no slip-resistance in the body. The structural backbone is explicit day milestones (Day 1, 7, 14, 30, 60) that give the viewer a reason to stay. Past-perfect framing at each milestone ("your arch HAS rebuilt") creates certainty rather than hope. Nurse persona established in every hook. Hospital B-roll anchors the pain context.

### Visual Direction
- **B-roll:** Nurse in hospital throughout (scrubs, hallways, patient rooms, concrete floors). Feet-focused shots at milestones. End-of-shift car park/locker room for resolution.
- **CGI:** Arch support cross-section animation at Day 1 (insole lifting arch) and Day 30 (arch structure under load distributing weight). Two cutaways max, 3-4s each.
- **Edit pace:** 2-3s shots. Milestone card holds 1.5s with a visual beat change. Progressive energy build from tired to confident.
- **Color:** Desaturated/cool for pre-product state (hooks, Day 1 pain context). Warming through milestones. Full warm color at Day 60 and close.
