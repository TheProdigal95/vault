## How It Works

1. **NMN converts to NAD+** — replenishing the cellular energy currency that declines with age
2. **Senolytics clear zombie cells** — removing damaged cells that drive chronic inflammation and tissue dysfunction
3. **Spermidine activates autophagy** — triggering the body's cellular recycling program to clear waste and build new components
4. **AMPK/SIRT1 activation** — turning on the same longevity pathways that caloric restriction and exercise activate
5. **Glycine + Taurine rebuild foundations** — providing the raw materials for cellular repair and detoxification

"After age 40, your cells stop cleaning up after themselves. Damaged cells pile up, energy production drops, and your body's repair systems slow down. Longevity targets all of this — it boosts your cellular energy (NAD+), clears out the damaged cells (senolytics), turns on your body's recycling system (autophagy), and provides the building blocks for repair (glycine + taurine)."

- **Federal Court Victory (November 2024):** U.S. District Court granted NPA's motion to halt FDA enforcement against NMN supplements. Court found FDA "vastly exceeded their authority" in retroactively applying drug preclusion rules.
- **Current Status:** FDA prohibited from pursuing enforcement against NMN products during ongoing legal stay. NMN can continue to be sold and marketed as dietary supplement.
- **FDA Response Timeline:** Originally due July 31, 2025; extended due to staffing reductions. Final decision expected no later than September 30, 2025.
- **Legal Precedent:** May set important precedent for other IND preclusion cases (NAC, CBD).
- **Market Impact:** The longer enforcement discretion continues, the stronger the legal position for NMN's supplement status.
