# Script Structure: Progression Timeline

The **Progression Timeline** is similar to the Benefits Timeline but focuses more heavily on the factual, day-by-day or month-by-month progression of a structural change. It often uses past-perfect framing ("Your arch HAS rebuilt") to create a sense of inevitable physiological outcome.

### **Core Breakdown**
- **Structure:** Explicit day milestones (Day 1, 7, 14, 30, 60, 90).
- **Framing:** Past-perfect milestone framing treats each benefit as an accomplished fact rather than a hope.
- **Visuals:** Uses CGI or anatomical overlays to show internal changes at key milestones.
- **Key Requirement:** Clear pain context in the hook, followed by a logical progression of relief.
- **Horizon:** Typically longer horizons (60-90 days) compared to benefit-only formats.
