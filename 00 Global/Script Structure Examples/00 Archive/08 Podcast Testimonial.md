## Brief 1 — Pattern Interrupt Podcast Testimonial

**Format:** Video — creator in podcast/interview setting, intercut with B-roll. Humor hook pivots to genuine testimonial.
**Persona:** Heavy Sleeper + Considerate Partner
**Structure:** UGC Podcast Testimonial
**Creator:** AI-generated male
**Close Type:** Emotional

### Concept

The viewer is a heavy sleeper whose alarm problem is affecting his relationship. This ad borrows Ad 02's proven format — a creator in a podcast/interview setting delivering a faux-complaint pattern interrupt, then pivoting to a rave testimonial. The humor hook earns 40 seconds of attention. The body weaves three extreme scenario proof points between the discovery story and the partner payoff. The failed alternatives are mentioned briefly (this is a testimonial, not an alternative parade — V3 handles that). Word-of-mouth discovery through a paramedic buddy anchors credibility to a profession where waking up on time is life-or-death.

### Footage Needs
- **Creator footage:** AI-generated male creator in podcast/interview setting (headphones, mic, casual but professional). Also needs a separate casual setting for Hook C (couch, bedroom, or kitchen — not the podcast setup).
- **Product B-roll:** Unboxing, band on wrist, close-up of band vibrating, close-up of digital display.
- **Lifestyle B-roll:** Person waking up calmly in bed, partner sleeping undisturbed beside them, morning light.
- **Failed alternatives (brief):** Phone showing multiple alarms, sunrise lamp on nightstand. Quick clips, 1-2 seconds each.

### Visual Direction
- **Tone:** Conversational and genuine. The creator talks like he's telling a friend a story he can't believe is true. Starts with mock outrage (hook), shifts to genuine enthusiasm (body), lands on quiet gratitude (close).
- **Podcast setting:** Clean background, good lighting, headphones and mic visible. The setting lends authority without being clinical.
- **Edit pace:** Fast — 2-3 second shots during B-roll intercuts. Podcast shots hold for 3-5 seconds when the creator is making a key point (extreme scenarios, partner payoff).
- **Caption style:** Clean sans-serif on semi-transparent dark blocks. All caps for emphasis words. Emoji used sparingly (one or two max).
- **B-roll intercuts:** Quick cuts between creator and B-roll every 5-8 seconds. Never more than 8 seconds on the creator without a visual break.
