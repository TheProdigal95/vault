## Script #9 — "Doctors Told These Women to Just Deal with It"

**Persona:** Older Women / Menopausal
**Structure:** UGC/story with narrative thread
**Duration:** ~49 seconds
**Word count:** ~125 words (VO at ~153 wpm)
**Caption style:** Native Instagram-style: semi-transparent block background, casual look. VoC quotes displayed as text overlays between clips.

### Concept

Creator narrates real customer stories displayed on screen. She's not pretending to be them. She reads and reacts to their reviews, adding commentary. The narrative arc: dismissed by doctors → discovered IM8 → got results. Reviews appear as text overlays while the creator walks the viewer through each woman's experience. The creator is the guide, the reviews are the proof.
