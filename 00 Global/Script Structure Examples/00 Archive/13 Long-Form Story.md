### Visual Direction
- **~2:28 long-form story modeled on Ref 5 (~2:53).** Narrator is the anchor — she carries the story direct-to-camera across three settings (dim bedroom, kitchen table, warm porch). B-roll inserts punctuate specific beats. The reference spends nearly half its runtime on Discovery + Mechanism — our Discovery (45s) and Why Elevate (25s) are the emotional core. Shame-to-pride arc runs through the full script: she stops telling people → hides the box → doesn't tell anyone → coworker asks → sister asks → "went from not telling anyone to wanting to tell everyone."
- Caption style: White sans-serif on semi-transparent dark strip. Captions are punchy reinforcements (3-6 words), not VO subtitles. Price captions shift to bolder treatment.
- Edit pace: Hook = warm, steady, 4-5s holds. Before = quicker B-roll inserts (2-3s) with held talking-head shots (4-6s). Discovery = building momentum, shorter cuts (3-5s), skepticism→surprise→emotion — hold the provider call turning point. Why Elevate = informational, steady (6s). Experience = warm, expanding (5-7s). Close = direct, clean.
