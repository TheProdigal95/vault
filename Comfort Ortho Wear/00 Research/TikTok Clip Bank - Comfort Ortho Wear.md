## Briefing usage rules

- Prefer **hands/feet/ground only** clips for DIRECT usage. If a face appears, either avoid that section, crop to feet/hands, or recreate.
- Keep original TikTok links as references in briefing docs; do not reference local downloads.
- Blur handles, logos, and visible branded marks when using direct source footage.
- For travel concepts, prioritize: wet streets, cobblestones, suitcase/packing, airport/plane, hotel-room fatigue, emergency shoe replacement.
- For nurse/long-shift concepts, prioritize: swollen feet, foot massage, slip tests, break-room shoe removal, hospital-floor walking.
- Do not reuse the same direct clip across too many scripts in a batch unless the reuse is deliberate and assigned to separate hook variants.
