## Offer Structure (as of 2026-04-20)

**Entry:** Free 7-day trial (priced as low as $0.99 in some in-app flows; trial terms variable)

**Subscription:**
- Standard: $188 USD per month (monthly recurring) — confirmed by client as of 2026-04-20
- Previous control: $188/3-month period (~$63/month effective). Client tested yearly, 6-month, 3-month, and 2-month cycles; $188/month produced highest revenue.

**Financial assistance:** Program available for those who cannot afford standard pricing. Application via help center. Process described as "simple and discreet."

**FSA/HSA eligible:** Yes, as of 2024. Instructions via help center.

**Guarantee / refund:** 30-day money-back guarantee, "no questions asked." Submit ticket at help center with receipt. Refund processed within 3 business days. (Note: several negative reviews dispute the ease of this process — see Brand Context vulnerabilities.)

**Free shipping:** N/A (digital product)

**Cancellation:** Via "Manage Subscription" in app Settings, or via help center ticket. Multiple reviews report difficulty with PayPal-based billing paths (subscription not auto-cancelling when app is deleted).

### Program mechanics (from site content + FAQ)

**Onboarding:**
1. Take free insomnia quiz (symptoms, life factors affecting sleep)
2. Get personalized CBT-I program
3. Feel refreshed every morning again

**Core program structure:**
- Daily micro-lessons (5–10 minutes per day)
- Intelligent algorithm analyzes sleep patterns and automatically adjusts the bedtime window for deeper slumber
- Sleep diary / daily sleep tracking
- Accountability "coaches" — user-designated people in their life to help stick with the program
- Sleep badges (progress gamification)

**Core techniques included (verbatim from site):**
- SRT: Sleep restriction therapy — restricting the sleep window to consolidate sleep drive
- Paradoxical intentions — intentionally trying to stay awake to reduce sleep effort
- Cognitive restructuring — addressing unhelpful thoughts about sleep
- Sleep anxiety management
- Progressive muscle relaxation, breathing exercises, meditation (relaxation module)
- Soundscapes, sleep music, bedtime stories (wind-down library)

**Program duration:** Customized per user; "a large majority start seeing noticeable improvements within the first 2 weeks." Content is heavy in weeks 1–4; several reviews note content becomes sparse after ~26–30 days.
