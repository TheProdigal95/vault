# Script Structure: Selfie Walk + Text Wall

Single continuous selfie walk with no VO. Trending or inspirational audio carries the emotional energy. Persistent on-screen text cards tell the entire arc — the text IS the ad. 3-5 atmospheric shots total; text tells the story, B-roll sets the mood. Only the opening hook card changes across A/B/C variations; body captions are identical.

### **Core Breakdown**
- **Structure:** Hook text card (varies per A/B/C) → body text cards stack over continuous walk → CTA card with payment logos.
- **VO register:** None. No voiceover. Audio is trending IG/TikTok audio or inspirational music bed. The text carries the message.
- **Time to benefit:** Immediate — caption IS the hook from frame 1.
- **Rhythm:** Single continuous take, slow and intimate, no cuts. Text cards appear as previous stay or fade. 5-8 words max per card, 2-3 cards per shot.
- **Duration:** 15-25s.
- **Close type:** None separate — CTA folds into the final body caption ("START THE QUIZ", "SEE IF YOU QUALIFY") with payment logos (Affirm, Afterpay, Klarna).
- **Visual grammar:** Single-modality by design. Creator-filmed selfie walk — one continuous shot, no cuts. Start with hesitancy/vulnerability, transition to confidence as the walk continues. Morning light, warm, natural.
- **Hook defaults:** Failed alternatives stack (❌ KETO / ❌ CALORIE COUNTING / ❌ MEAL REPLACEMENTS). Timeline progression (YEAR 1: X / YEAR 3: Y / YEAR 5: solution). Identity statement ("I stopped looking in mirrors").
- **Caption strategy:** Medium sans-serif on semi-transparent grey. Text wall stacking — all text visible from start to end. Shame-to-pride arc: struggle → discovery → transformation → payoff.

### When to Use
- When the product addresses something the buyer is ashamed of or has been hiding
- When emotional resonance matters more than mechanism education
- When the target audience consumes IG Reels / TikTok natively (they recognize the selfie walk format)
- Short-form placements (15-25s) where VO would feel forced

### Key Rules
- **No VO.** The text carries the entire arc. Audio is mood, not message.
- **Single continuous take.** No cuts. The walk IS the format. Exception: opening shot can be a mirror/porch/indoor beat that transitions to the walk.
- **Caption compression:** Cut connective tissue ("I kept searching," "I signed up that night"). Keep specific numbers, key proof points, and emotional payoff lines.
- **3-5 atmospheric shots TOTAL.** Each shot holds multiple text cards. This is NOT B-roll + captions (one shot per caption = montage, not text wall).
- **Only hooks vary.** Body captions and walk footage are identical across A/B/C/D. The hook card is the only variable.
- **Format Note:** Mark "Single-modality (Selfie Walk) intentional per format" in Visual Direction to prevent QA flagging the lack of modality variety.
