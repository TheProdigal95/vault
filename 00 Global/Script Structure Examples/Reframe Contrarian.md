# Script Structure: Reframe Contrarian

The **Reframe Contrarian** format challenges the viewer's existing beliefs or common industry advice. It identifies a "failed hack" or a misunderstood symptom and provides a logic-led pivot to a new mechanism (the product's solve).

### **Core Breakdown**
- **Hook Strategy:** Problem-only hooks that validate the viewer's frustration with current solutions.
- **The Pivot:** A sharp "It's not X, it's Y" moment that externalizes the blame (e.g., "It's not aging, it's nutrient depletion").
- **Logic-Led:** Uses scientific reasoning or analogies to explain why the product succeeds where others fail.
- **Tone:** Direct, slightly challenging, and authoritative.
