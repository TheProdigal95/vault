## Brief 01 — The Secret She Couldn't Keep (Comic Book)

**Persona:** Cost-conscious medical weight loss seeker  
**Structure:** Emotional arc  
**Platform:** Meta static  

Reach Digital_Elevate_Emotional_Secret She Couldn't Keep_01_T006

### References

**Visual Treatment**
- Reference to be added by strategist.
  - Use for comic panel styling, character treatment, and speech bubble placement.

### Image Direction
- **Image to generate:** Four-panel comic book static showing one woman moving from secrecy to pride.
  - Panel 1: sitting alone in bed at night, phone glow on her face, reading an Elevate eligibility screen with nervous curiosity.
  - Panel 2: hiding her phone slightly when someone walks into the room, trying to act normal.
  - Panel 3: outside in daylight, walking with coffee, visibly lighter and more at ease while checking her phone.
  - Panel 4: smiling on the couch, texting a friend the link with excited "you need to see this" energy.
- **Style:** cute comic book / illustrated ad style. It can lean playful and charming.
- **Typography:** headline and supporting copy outside the vignettes should be clean and modern, not comic-book/marvel-style lettering.
- **Compliance:** emotional arc only. No before/after body contrast, drug imagery, weight numbers, or time-based results.

### Speech Bubbles

- **Panel 1:** "Maybe this is it."
- **Panel 2:** "Can anyone tell?"
- **Panel 3:** "I feel supported."
- **Panel 4:** "I'm sending you the link."

### Headline Variations
- I didn't want anyone to know. Now I want to tell everyone.
- Diets failed you. Medicine won't.
- Maybe willpower was never the problem.

### Design Elements
- **Subheading:** "Medical Weight Loss from $58/week. No insurance needed."
- **Payment logos:** Affirm, Afterpay, Klarna above the CTA.
- **CTA:** "See if you qualify."
