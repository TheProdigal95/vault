## Raw Notes — Batch Strategy

T001 data is early but directional. "Text Wall" formats are the clear winners on both spend and attention (CTR). The Harvard hero quote card is the strongest CTR driver for statics. 

For T002, we are moving from "Static-to-Video Hybrids" to **Actual Video Scripts** for the editing team, while doubling down on the text-wall density that's working.

**Core Directives for T002:**
- **Upgraded Text Walls:** Move from static backgrounds to moving AI B-roll (cinematic scene transitions, subtle movements) to increase "felt quality" while keeping the text-wall hook density.
- **The Sleep Anxiety Spiraler:** This is our biggest white space. We're launching a dedicated "Anxiety Feedback Loop" mechanism explainer to target the 16% of users who are stuck in the "anxious about sleep → can't sleep" loop.
- **Mechanism Depth:** Move beyond just "Harvard-backed" to explaining *how* it works (Sleep Restriction, Stimulus Control, Cognitive Reconditioning). 
- **Diverse Closes:** Move away from 50% Authority closes. Test more "Outcome-Anchored" and "Benefit-led" closes (e.g., "Wake up rested").
- **Persona Expansion:** Adding the **Overwhelmed New Parent** (client-confirmed segment) with a dedicated guilt/outcomes angle.

## Script Criteria

### Brand-Specific Overrides (Carried forward from T001)

- **"Founded and developed at Harvard"** — Use verbatim. Harvard-only — no Brigham in creative.
- **Clinical outcome stats — exact phrasing only:**
  - "Over 80% improve sleep"
  - "74 minute increase in total sleep time"
  - "53 minutes less time to fall asleep"
  - "52% fewer awakenings"
  - **Editor screenshot source (all four stats):** `https://stellarsleep.com/learn/why-stellar-sleep`
- **Mechanism name:** CBT-I (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia).
- **No discount/promo framing.**
- **"Chronic insomnia"** = trouble sleeping 3+ nights per week for 3+ months.
- **FSA/HSA eligible** — safe to use.
- **Expert-body recommendation:** "CBT-I is the first-line treatment for chronic insomnia, recommended by the American College of Physicians and the American Academy of Sleep Medicine."
- **"Same psychology-based techniques as top sleep clinics"** — approved soft-authority phrase.
- **"Just 10 minutes a day"** — approved close-card tag.
- **No brand / competitor / pharmaceutical product names.**
- **Stellar Sleep Top Spender references = AI 3D character DNA only.**
- **NO FLYING / FLOATING PEOPLE:** Hard rejection from client on "hallucinatory" physics where people are literally floating or flying. Dreamy, ethereal, and fantasy metaphors (e.g., maze, piggy bank, smoke-clouds) are **highly encouraged**—just keep the characters themselves grounded.

### Known Vulnerabilities

- **Pricing opacity + billing friction.** Avoid free trial / cancel-anytime framing.
- **SRT backlash.** Don't promise immediate relief. "Start seeing results in 2 weeks."
- **Content depth after month 1.**
