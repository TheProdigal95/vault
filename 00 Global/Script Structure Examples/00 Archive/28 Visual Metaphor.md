**Persona:** The Chronic Everything-Failer (CEF)

### Concept
This script uses the **"Sleep Debt Piggy Bank"** visual metaphor to explain the cumulative nature of insomnia. It reframes the problem from simple fatigue to a "biological debt" that temporary hacks cannot repay, leading directly into the CBT-I solution for long-term reconditioning.

### Visual Direction
- **Footage approach:** Cinematic AI-generated video. High contrast between the "dark sand" of the debt and the "golden light" of the recovery.
- **Color:** Deep blues and greys for the debt/night sections; transitions to warm amber and gold for the outcome.
- **Edit pace:** Slow and weighted during the metaphor/pain beats; speeds up slightly during the mechanism reveal; holds on the final aspirational shot.
