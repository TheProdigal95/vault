## Meta Account Flags

**Health & Wellness flag** — The account has been flagged for health & wellness content. This previously caused the team to stop running ads targeting arthritis, diabetic, and chronic joint pain personas due to potential medical claim concerns. With Blotout edge tag now implemented, Sam believes these personas can be tested again — but exercise caution with medical claims in copy.

---

## Competitor Brand Mentions

**Sketchers trademark incident (Nov 2025):** Editor forgot to blur the Sketchers logo in a video ad. Resulted in a trademark issue.

**Rules:**
- **Video:** Blur all competitor brand logos visible in any clip (TikTok pulls, YouTube clips, B-roll). This includes Sketchers, Hoka, New Balance, or any footwear brand logo.
- **On-screen text:** If typing a competitor brand name, replace one letter with an asterisk (e.g., "Sk*tchers", "H*ka"). Do not spell competitor brand names in full on screen.
- **Voiceover:** Competitor brand names CAN be spoken in voiceover — no restrictions on audio mentions.

---

## Authority Claims

- **"Podiatrist approved" — AVOID.** No current podiatrist endorsement or certification.
- **"Doctor recommended" — gray area.** Previously had a doctor ambassador (not a foot specialist); no longer active. The website uses this claim but Sam flagged it as "a bit more on the gray side."
- **"Expert recommended" — acceptable.** Sam approved this phrasing on the call.
- **No US certifications** for the product. A Chinese certification exists but is not relevant for US market claims.

---

## Creative Asset Sourcing

- When using clips from TikTok, YouTube, or any user-generated platform, blur all visible brand logos (not just footwear — any brand that could trigger a trademark claim).
- Sam's team also produces creative weekly; their assets go into a shared Google Drive folder organized by week.
