CoQ10

CoQ10 is a naturally occurring antioxidant that declines with age. It is also found to be lower in people with heart disease and in people who use statins. CoQ10 supplementation has been shown in some studies to reduce the risk of muscle aches associated with statin use, improve physical performance and may help reduce blood pressure and risk of migraines.
