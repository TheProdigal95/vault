Furthermore, in addition to the general lack of evidence to substantiate the health
benefit claims Oral Only makes regarding its compounded oral testosterone product, Oral Only
is unable to demonstrate that its products are bioavailable, which casts doubt on the efficacy of
the product to even deliver the “testosterone” to the user. Our understanding is that Oral Only
uses testosterone base as its active ingredient. Unmodified testosterone administered orally is
well established in the scientific literature to have very low systemic bioavailability due to
extensive first-pass metabolism, with studies demonstrating bioavailability of only 3.56%. 19
Although testosterone is absorbed from the gastrointestinal tract, it is rapidly metabolized and
inactivated in the intestinal wall and liver before reaching systemic circulation in meaningful
quantities. For this reason, oral administration of unmodified testosterone has historically been
considered an ineffective route of delivery, and no FDA-approved testosterone product relies on
unmodified testosterone base for oral administration. FDA-approved oral TRT KYZATREX®
avoids this issue by using testosterone undecanoate, as KYZATREX® was designed to promote
lymphatic absorption and reduce first-pass hepatic metabolism. By contrast, Oral Only’s
product contains unmodified testosterone base, with no demonstrated lymphatic absorption
pathway, and it has not been proven to be safe and effective.
