# Medical Assistant

**General guidelines**

- Any internal data we use (certification rates, graduation rates, etc) should be backed up by actual data that is from a recent and reasonable time period   
- Any external data we use should link to proper sources / citations   
- Any employer logos we use must be places where graduates of the program with no prior affiliation with the employer were hired   
- Any reviews should be from real students   
- Any comparison to competitors should be accurate and grounded in real, publicly available data   
- Any time estimates (e.g. time to complete the program) should be accurate and reflect the actual length of the program 

**What we cannot do** 

- Refer to our program as a Certified Medical Assistant program   
  - The program qualifies students to take a certification exam, but the program itself is not certified   
- Make any guarantees of employment   
- Make any guarantees of externship placement within a certain time period   
- Insinuate that our program is accredited   
- Use the term money back guarantee unless it is referring to the 72 hour period where students can cancel and get their registration fee back

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Enroll in our Certified Dental Assistant program  
* We're a Certified Dental Assistant training program  
* Our CDA program is certified  
* Stepful is a certified training provider  
* DANB-certified Dental Assistant program (DANB certifies individuals, not programs)

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* Program prepares you for the Radiation Health and Safety (RHS) exam from the Dental Assisting National Board (DANB)  
* Training program that helps you earn your DANB-RHS certification  
* Qualify to take the DANB Radiation Health and Safety (RHS) exam  
* Become a certified Dental Assistant (referring to the person becoming certified, not the program)  
* Graduate ready to sit for the DANB RHS exam

**2\. ACCREDITATION LANGUAGE**

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Accredited Dental Assistant program  
* Our program is accredited by...  
* Stepful is an accredited institution  
* Receive an accredited certificate  
* Nationally accredited training

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* Prepares you for the DANB RHS exam, issued by the Dental Assisting National Board, an accredited certifying body  
* Earn a nationally recognized certification from an accredited organization  
* While Stepful is not institutionally accredited, our curriculum prepares you for the DANB RHS credential, which is recognized by employers nationwide

**3\. EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEES**

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Guaranteed job placement  
* We guarantee you'll find employment  
* 100% job placement  
* Get hired or your money back  
* Land a job in 30 days  
* We'll get you a job at \[Company Name\]

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* We help you secure a full-time job   
* Job coaching to support your search

**4\. EXTERNSHIP PLACEMENT TIMING**

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Guaranteed externship placement within 30 days  
* We place all students in externships  
* You will be placed in an externship by \[specific date\]  
* Immediate externship placement

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* Stepful supports securing interview opportunities at a local dental practice, typically within 1–3 months of passing the national exam  
* Placement is subject to site availability and student eligibility

**5\. INTERNAL DATA & STATISTICS**

**Requirements before publishing any stat:**

* Must have actual data from recent cohorts (last 6–12 months)  
* Must specify the time period (e.g., ‘X% pass rate based on Jan–Dec 2024 graduates’)  
* Sample size must be meaningful  
* Job placement rate must include clear conditions and date range

**Additional data claims to avoid:**

* Students see an average salary increase of X% (unless you have before/after data)  
* Our graduates make more than other programs (unless you have comparative data)  
* 100% of our students pass on the first try

**Placeholder language to use until data is confirmed:**

* \[PASS RATE DATA NEEDED — must include time period and sample size before publishing\]  
* \[JOB PLACEMENT DATA NEEDED — must include conditions and date range before publishing\]

**6\. EXTERNAL DATA & CITATIONS**

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Dental Assistants earn up to $X without a source  
* The dental industry is growing without citing BLS or similar  
* Our program is better than \[Competitor\] without data

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (2024), Dental Assistants earn a median annual salary of $47,300  
* The BLS projects \[X\]% job growth for Dental Assistants through \[year\] (verify current projections at bls.gov)  
* Link to sources: BLS occupational outlook (https://www.bls.gov/ooh/healthcare/dental-assistants.htm), DANB state requirements, etc.

**Best practices:**

* Always link to or cite government sources (BLS, state dental boards, DANB)  
* For industry statistics, use reputable dental or healthcare publications  
* For competitor comparisons, only cite publicly available information (their websites, reviews, etc.)

**7\. EMPLOYER LOGOS**

**Requirements:**

* **Only** use logos where Stepful graduates with NO prior affiliation were hired  
* **Must** be actual placements, not just we applied there  
* **Keep records** of which graduates were hired at each employer

❌ **NEVER:**

* Show a logo if a student only did their externship there (not hired)  
* Show a logo if the student already worked there before Stepful  
* Show a logo if you only sent resumes but no one was hired

**8\. COMPETITOR COMPARISONS**

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* We're better than \[Competitor Name\]  
* Unlike \[Competitor\], we guarantee jobs  
* Other programs take 12 months, ours only takes 4 (without verification)  
* \[Competitor\] has a lower pass rate (unless publicly stated by them)

✅ **SAY INSTEAD:**

* Our 4-month program includes... (focus on your features, not comparison)  
* We offer live classes four times weekly (state your facts, let users compare)  
* If comparing, only use publicly available data from their websites or reviews  
* Many programs require in-person attendance; Stepful is 100% online for the first 4 months

**9\. TIME ESTIMATES**

**Current program details:**

* 4-month (16-week) online program  
* 80 in-person clinical hours available   
* Students who pursue the externship typically begin within 1–3 months of passing their national exam

✅ **Your time estimates are good when they:**

* Reflect actual program length (4 months, 16 weeks online)  
* Describe clinical hours as optional  
* Note that some states may have additional licensing requirements (check DANB state requirements)  
* Use ‘typically’ for variable timelines

❌ **AVOID:**

* Become a Dental Assistant in 4 months (students aren’t certified until after passing the DANB RHS exam)  
* Complete everything in 5 months total (doesn’t account for exam scheduling and state-specific requirements)  
* Externship is required (it is not, for this program)

✅ **MORE ACCURATE:**

* 4-month online program that prepares you for the DANB RHS exam  
* An optional 80-hour in-person externship is available to gain hands-on experience  
* Note: state dental assistant requirements vary; some states require clinical hours for employment or licensure

**10\. MONEY BACK GUARANTEE LANGUAGE**

❌ **ONLY USE MONEY BACK GUARANTEE FOR:**

* The 3-day cancellation period  
* The 2-week refund after classes begin (for tuition only)

✅ **YOUR CURRENT LANGUAGE:**

* 3-day money back guarantee ✓  
* Specifying what's refundable (tuition vs. enrollment fees) ✓

❌ **NEVER SAY:**

* Money back guarantee if you don't get hired  
* Get your money back if you don't pass the exam  
* Satisfaction guaranteed or your money back (unless specifically referring to the 3-day window)
