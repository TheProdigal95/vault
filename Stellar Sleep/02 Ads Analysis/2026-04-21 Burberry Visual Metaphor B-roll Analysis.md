### E. Dreamy dancing motion quality

The movement language that separates this ad from generic "flying" footage. Every shot above follows these four hallmarks — repeat them in any AI prompt to inherit the feel:

1. **Arms out / arms reaching** — never fists, never pointed Superman-style. Arms extend sideways like a ballet dancer or a swan dive. Reads *receptive*, not *propulsive*.
2. **Arched / curved spines** — never rigid. The body is a soft curve; extension comes from the torso. Separates *dancer* from *action figure*.
3. **Slow-motion holds between beats** — the pause is part of the dance. Mid-move frames have a *held* quality, like a photograph of a dancer mid-leap.
4. **Partner interactions are reaches, not grabs** — fingers extended toward each other rather than clasping. Reads *connection*, not *dependence*.

**AI-prompt fragments that trigger this quality:**
- `"ballet dancer mid-leap, arms extended, body in a soft arch"` (not "flying figure," not "superhero pose")
- `"reaching toward each other with fingertips, not quite touching"` (not "holding," not "grabbing")
- `"slow-motion hold on a weightless body curve"` (not "action pose")
- `"suspended mid-movement like a photograph of a dance"` (not "frozen in flight")

## Section 3 — Music / sound design read

- **Genre / mood:** Modern classical / cinematic orchestral blended with subtle ambient electronic texture. Mood escalates from mystery → wonder → transcendent joy → serene resolution.
- **Instrumentation:** Lush string section (violins, violas, cellos) dominant. Solo violin carries the lyrical main melody. Ambient synth pads for drone and texture. Diegetic naturalistic sound design at the start (wind, rustling) before the score takes over completely.
- **Tempo + dynamics:**
  - **Intro (0:23–0:32)** — low quiet ambient drone (pianissimo).
  - **Build (0:32–0:51)** — wind "whoosh" acts as a percussive entry cue, then solo violin. Steady moderate tempo begins a long crescendo as more people take flight.
  - **Climax (0:51–1:54)** — dense complex overlapping string arpeggios, powerful sustained chords coinciding with the most dynamic collective flying sequences. Dynamics from forte to fortissimo.
  - **Resolution (1:54–2:08)** — pulls back to ethereal territory. Final single long peaceful chord fades slowly to nothing.
- **How the sound reinforces the feeling:**
  - **The transition cue** — the gust of wind at 0:30 that physically lifts the first person is immediately answered by the solo violin at 0:32. This sonically signals the shift from natural law to dream-logic.
  - **Ascension and melody** — soaring violin melody mirrors the physical act of flying. Pitch rises on leaps, sustained on holds, arpeggios drive the "swimming through air" sequences.
  - **Emotional swells** — crescendos are timed to collective actions (group flight, cliff-edge convergence). The score reinforces "shared" freedom, not just individual.
  - **The resolution** — ending on a quiet, sustained, fade-out chord over the cliff-embrace gives a feeling of peaceful *completion* rather than dramatic climax. That is the specific feeling we want to borrow for the sleep metaphor.

## Section 4 — How to abstract for AI generation

Four signature ingredients — strip the Burberry branding, keep the feeling-makers, and we can generate many variants to test for Ad 9.

- **Weightless horizontal slow-motion movement.** The core visual is human figures moving gracefully and *horizontally* through the air, just above a natural surface (field, forest floor, water). Prompt with "slow-motion," "floating," "drifting," "tumbling" — never "flying" alone, which defaults to a superhero look. The key is *defying gravity gently*, dance-like.
    - *Example fragment:* "cinematic slow-motion shot of a person floating horizontally just above a golden wheat field, body in a relaxed weightless pose, drifting."
- **Expansive unspoiled natural landscapes with diffused lighting.** Freedom is amplified by open settings (fields, coasts, forests, lakes). Lighting should be soft and ethereal — overcast daylight, golden hour, moody dusk. No harsh shadows. Painterly, surreal quality. Environment is the co-star.
    - *Example fragment:* "immense empty landscape of rolling green hills meeting a calm sea, soft overcast light, ethereal mood, painterly."
- **Dynamic low-angle tracking cinematography.** Camera is a participant, not an observer. Use "low-angle tracking shot," "drone shot skimming the ground," "sweeping orbital shot," "dynamic tracking alongside" — build momentum + shared immersion with the subject.
    - *Example fragment:* "dynamic low-angle tracking shot following a person as they float weightlessly through a dense pine forest, dappled light."
- **Collective / interconnected motion.** The emotional payoff is *shared*. Single flying figures read cool; multiple figures interacting in mid-air read like *freedom and connection*. Prompt with "flying in formation," "dancing together in the air," "reaching for each other while floating," "converging in a weightless embrace."
    - *Example fragment:* "two figures floating and spinning around each other in slow motion above a golden field, reaching for each other, sun-drenched golden-hour light."

### Translation note for the Stellar Sleep Ad 9 brief

The Burberry ad is *all* dreamy. For Ad 9, the dreamy sequences should appear *after* a 3am bedroom reality snap — in-rhythm:

1. **Reality (0–4s):** 3am bedroom, restless in bed, dim warm lamp. (Diegetic: clock tick, breath.)
2. **Dream-escape (4–18s):** Weightless horizontal float + group/shared connection beats + natural-landscape diffused light. (Score: solo string enters, crescendo into group scene.)
3. **Snap back to reality (18–20s):** Sharp cut back to 3am bed — the dream was the missing thing. (Score: silence or single held note.)
4. **Resolution (20–30s):** Dreamy returns, but now earned — subhead reveals it as CBT-I / Stellar Sleep outcome. Harvard proof tag in final frame. (Score: resolves into sustained peaceful chord like the Burberry ending.)

The category is **"visual metaphor b-roll — dreamy escape sequence"**, where *escape from restlessness* is the emotional metaphor and weightless floating over natural landscapes is the visual ingredient that carries it.
