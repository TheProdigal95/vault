## Cross-Ad Patterns

### What's working
- **TRT angle is the most efficient buyer driver.** Three of the top 5 ads by purchase volume are TRT-specific. #2 (TRT Timeline) has the best CPP in account at $561. #17 (Don't Skip) has ROAS 9.30 with minimal spend — almost certainly getting scaled.
- **Process/timeline static format outperforms offer statics.** Ad #2 (TRT Timeline) has no people, no hero image, just a clean 4-step roadmap. Lower CTR (1.25%) but best CPP by 2x over the next best. Proves that clarity of process converts better than urgency of price.
- **Warning hook + loss-aversion framing beats curiosity hook.** Ad #17 vs #16 is a clean A/B on the same creative — "Don't skip this step" at ROAS 9.30 vs "if your energy sucks" at ROAS 0.29. The 🚨 pattern interrupt + "don't skip" loss frame is doing the heavy lifting.
- **$49 TRT entry point is the right threshold.** Multiple ads using "Start for $49" are converting. The price appears at the right consideration level for TRT-curious men.

### What's broken
- **High CTR / no purchase pattern is consistent across 6 ads.** Ads #5, #6, #7, #11, #13 all have CTRs of 3-7.5% and zero purchases. These aren't hook problems — they're landing page or audience signal problems. The comparison and editorial formats are generating genuine interest that isn't being captured post-click.
- **Tony Robbins SALE assets are fatiguing.** #1 has been running for 3+ weeks. T003 it drove $14.5K / 12 purchases at $1,208 CPP. Now $10.9K / 10 purchases at $1,088 CPP. Still converting but the efficiency curve is declining.
- **BOF sale statics ($199 offer) are expensive at this stage.** $1,664 and $1,535 CPP on retargeting — there are still conversions coming in, but cost has risen significantly. The audience has been hit many times.

### Angles to pursue in T004
- **Hormone Optimization (TRT-specific)** — most efficient angle by CPP; underexplored in terms of formats and hooks
- **"Feel & Perform Like Yourself"** — dominant by spend share, still scaling; the emotional identity-recovery hook is the right territory
- **The "Don't Skip" pattern** — the ROAS 9.30 result on a $166 test is a breakout signal; expand the concept beyond the current protagonist version

### Format signals for T004
- **Process/Timeline static** — replicate the TRT Timeline format (#2) for the Core offer (not just TRT)
- **UGC-style protagonist video (Hormone/TRT angle)** — #17's structure is clearly working; multiple variations of the "I did everything right + discovered testosterone" story
- **Comparison format** — #11's 7.51% CTR proves the PCP comparison angle lands; needs a landing page fix or a format change to convert, not a new hook
- **"Don't Skip" hook variations** — test the warning pattern on other pain points beyond low T (bloodwork, biological age, proactive testing)
