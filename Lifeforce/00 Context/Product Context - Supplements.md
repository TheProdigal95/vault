## Supplement Strategy Notes for Creative

1. **Supplements are not sold in isolation — they're part of the clinical ecosystem.** Every product page connects to biomarkers. The pitch is: "Your blood tells us what you need." This is fundamentally different from typical DTC supplement marketing.

2. **Member discount is the conversion lever.** 30% off supplement subscriptions means a member taking 3 supplements saves $50-$100+/month on supplements alone — which partially offsets the $149/month membership cost. Creative should emphasize "your membership pays for itself."

3. **Peak Rest is the gateway product.** Sold on TonyRobbins.com, available without membership, solves a universal problem (sleep). Many members enter via Peak Rest → discover biomarker testing → become full members.

4. **Proprietary ingredients are the differentiation story.** NAD3, CLOCK Complex, Magtein, K2Vital, SmartPrime-Om, Wellmune, ResistAid — these are not available in generic supplements. The "you can't buy this elsewhere" angle matters for informed buyers.

5. **Biomarker targeting is unique.** Each product page lists which biomarkers it targets. No other supplement brand connects individual products to specific blood test markers this clearly.
