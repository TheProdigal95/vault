# Visual Modality Reference

Visual modalities are how the footage is assembled and presented. They layer on top of script structures — an Authority Rating can be shot as Talking Head, Green Screen + PiP, or AI Animated Character. A Failed-Remedies Cascade can be Selfie/Couch or VO + B-Roll.

**Use in Working Documents:** When assigning a concept, specify both: "Structure: Authority Rating, Modality: Green Screen + PiP" — so the structure defines the argument and the modality defines the visual execution.

---

## The Modalities

### Talking Head (Direct to Camera)
Creator or authority on camera, speaking directly to the viewer. Single-camera, single location. The face carries the performance.
- **When to use:** Creator-led formats, authority formats, testimonials. When the face and delivery ARE the trust signal.
- **Visual column:** "AI UGC [type] faces camera, [expression]." Emotional arc descriptors belong here.
- **Modality mix:** Often combined with B-Roll Inserts (creator stays in frame, B-roll cutaways prove claims).
- **Production:** Phone-quality, natural lighting, no color grading. Direct eye contact.

### Green Screen + PiP (Picture-in-Picture)
Creator or authority composited over B-roll footage. Alternates between full-frame (direct address) and corner-box (B-roll proves the mechanism). The authority never fully leaves the screen.
- **When to use:** Authority formats where the expert needs to stay visible while proof footage plays. Educational formats where mechanism visualization needs screen space.
- **Visual column:** Lead with "GREEN SCREEN:" or "PiP:" to signal modality. Specify "full-frame" or "corner-box" per beat.
- **Transition rule:** Full-frame for hook, close, and transitions. Corner-box during proof/demo B-roll. Alternate every 5-8s.
- **Production:** AI UGC or filmed talent on green screen. B-roll composited behind (full-frame) or alongside (corner-box).

### Green Screen Reaction (TK Clip Behind Reactor)
Organic TikTok clip plays as-is behind a reactor for 3-5s. Reactor watches, reacts visibly. Green screen drops, reactor takes over and the script begins.
- **When to use:** Hooks only. When an organic TikTok clip IS the scroll-stop and the authority's reaction adds credibility.
- **Visual column:** "GREEN SCREEN: Play [TK ref] behind [reactor type]. [Reactor] watches, [reaction]. Green screen drops."
- **Timing rule:** Clip plays for 4-5s minimum. Reactor must have time to visibly react. Green screen drop is its own beat — don't cram it into the same timestamp row as the clip.
- **Source rules:** Original clip plays as-is. Reactor is AI UGC. Blur logos/handles on the clip.

### TK Direct Clip (Organic Footage Insert)
Original TikTok footage used as-is in the ad — as a hook opener, body insert, or proof shot. POV shots, feet, hands, environment only. No identifiable creator face as focal point.
- **When to use:** When organic footage IS the proof and re-creating it would lose authenticity. Syringe drop tests, swollen feet, soaked shoes on cobblestones, cracked heels.
- **Visual column:** Lead with "[TK ref] DIRECT CLIP:" or "[TK ref] DIRECT @ [timestamp]:" and specify what the viewer sees.
- **Timing rule:** 4-5s minimum for scroll-stop clips. No VO for first 3-4s — let the visual land. "Hold the shot" direction for the editor.
- **Source rules:** Under 5K followers. POV/feet/hands/environment only. Blur logos and handles. Pre-approved clips noted in Editor Notes.

### VO + B-Roll (No On-Camera Talent)
Voiceover narrates over B-roll, product shots, stock footage, and overlays. No creator on screen. The VO carries the argument; the visuals prove it.
- **When to use:** Narrated humor, product demos, montage formats, any format where the visual evidence matters more than a face.
- **Visual column:** Specify B-roll shot list per beat. Source per clip (brand library, editor-source, stock). 2-3s cuts during body beats.
- **Production:** AI narrator VO or pre-recorded. B-roll from brand library, editor-sourced, or AI-generated.

### Selfie Walk (Continuous Single Take)
Creator-filmed continuous walk, no cuts. The walk IS the format — start vulnerable, end confident. Text cards stack over the footage. Usually paired with trending audio, no VO.
- **When to use:** Text Wall structure only. Shame-to-pride arcs. When the visual needs to feel raw and personal.
- **Visual column:** "Creator-filmed continuous selfie walk. [Starting emotion] shifting to [ending emotion]."
- **Production:** Real creator footage (not AI). One take, one shot. Morning light, natural.

### Persistent Overlays (Text-Heavy)
Large, persistent text cards, score graphics, verdict badges, or data overlays that stay on screen across multiple beats. The overlays are load-bearing — they carry information the viewer needs for sound-off comprehension.
- **When to use:** Rating formats (persistent red X / green check per competitor), countdown formats (numbered headers), investigation formats (TEST 1 / TEST 2 / TEST 3), any format where verdict captions accumulate.
- **Visual column:** Specify what persists: "Red X stays on screen," "Running total: $525 appears," "Persistent green check."
- **Often combined with:** Talking Head, Green Screen + PiP, VO + B-Roll. Persistent overlays are an additive layer, not a standalone modality.

### Physical Product Demo
Hands-on demonstration of the product's features. Thumb pressing foam, squeezing toe box, pushing sole on wet tile, pulling insole out. The demo IS the proof — no CGI needed.
- **When to use:** Three-Test Investigation, product reveal beats in any structure, any format where "show don't tell" is more credible than mechanism education.
- **Visual column:** Lead with the physical action: "Thumb presses arch firmly. It holds." Specify surface, lighting, angle.
- **Production:** Phone-quality, counter/table surface, natural lighting. Real product, not renders.

## Modality × Structure Matrix

Common pairings. Not exhaustive — new combinations emerge per batch.

| Structure | Primary Modality | Common Add-Ons |
|---|---|---|
| Authority Rating | Green Screen + PiP | Persistent Overlays, TK Direct Clips |
| Creator-Led Contrarian | Talking Head | TK Direct Clips, Green Screen Reaction |
| Countdown Ranking | Talking Head | Persistent Overlays, Physical Product Demo |
| Collection Failure | Talking Head | Persistent Overlays |
| Three-Test Investigation | Physical Product Demo | Talking Head, Persistent Overlays |
| Failed-Remedies Cascade | Talking Head | VO + B-Roll inserts |
| Consequence Narrative | Talking Head | TK Direct Clips, Green Screen Reaction |
| Selfie Walk + Text Wall | Selfie Walk | Persistent Overlays |
| AI Animated Character | Green Screen + PiP | Persistent Overlays |
| Narrated UGC Humor | VO + B-Roll | Persistent Overlays |
| Stack Replacement | Physical Product Demo | Persistent Overlays, VO + B-Roll |
| Progression Timeline | Green Screen + PiP or VO + B-Roll | Persistent Overlays |
| Numbered Reasons | Talking Head or VO + B-Roll | Persistent Overlays |
| Reframe Contrarian | Green Screen + PiP or Talking Head | — |
