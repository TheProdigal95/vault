## Script #7 — B-Roll + Caption — PF Resolution

**Persona:** Plantar Fasciitis & Heel Pain Sufferer
**Structure:** B-roll + persistent caption (no voiceover, music only)
**Duration:** ~15 seconds
**Caption style:** Single persistent native caption (conversational, typed-over-video feel)
**Close type:** n/a (format has no traditional close — CTA is implicit in caption + end card)

### Concept

Zero-production, native-feeling ad modeled directly on Reference 1 (Orthofeet B-Roll + Caption). The entire creative is B-roll of a nurse walking comfortably through her hospital shift, with a single persistent caption overlay that does all the persuasion. No voiceover, no talking head, no product demo. Background music only (110-120 BPM upbeat instrumental). The caption mimics someone typing a casual comment over their own video — testimonial language with a specific outcome statement. This format is completely untested in the ComfortWear account and tests whether a native-feeling, zero-production ad can convert cold traffic.

### Visual Direction
- **B-roll:** Nurse in scrubs walking hospital corridors comfortably. Feet shots on hospital tile floors. Hallway walk shots from behind and beside. End-of-shift moment leaving building with energy (not limping). All footage warm, well-lit, natural. No talking head, no product hero shots (shoe visible on feet only).
- **Edit pace:** Slow and steady. 3-4s shots. No fast cuts. Comfortable, confident energy. Matches the "life is good now" tone of the caption.
- **Color:** Full warm color throughout. No desaturation, no problem-state visuals. This is pure after-state.
- **Audio:** 110-120 BPM upbeat instrumental. No voiceover. Music carries the scroll-stop alongside the caption.
- **Caption placement:** Single text block, persistent on screen for the full duration. Positioned lower-third, native social caption style (white text, slight shadow, left-aligned). Appears immediately on first frame.
