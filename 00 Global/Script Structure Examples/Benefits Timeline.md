# Script Structure: Benefits Timeline

The **Benefits Timeline** is a high-retention format that tracks a user's transformation over time (typically 30, 60, or 90 days). It uses explicit day milestones to give the viewer a reason to stay and "earn" the next benefit reveal.

### **Core Breakdown**
- **Time to Benefit:** 5-10 seconds. Must open with an immediate win ("from your first sip").
- **Duration:** 45-60 seconds.
- **Key Requirement:** 3-4 milestones max. Each milestone names a specific ingredient or biological mechanism. 
- **Framing:** Use "honest starts" (e.g., Day 1 is often relief/sensation, while Day 90 is structural change). Use days instead of weeks for more granular momentum.
- **Tone:** Upbeat and factual.
