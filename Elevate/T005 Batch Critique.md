## Cross-Batch Patterns

1. **T005 is intentionally concentrated around the conversion kit.** Price, $0 down, payment plans, eligibility, and no-insurance framing are repeated because they are the account's strongest L7 signals.

2. **Hook-to-body transitions have been normalized.** The body opener now answers the hook premise before moving into logistics, especially in text-wall scripts where the first body card has to carry the bridge.

3. **Benefits timelines are the main strategic/compliance question.** If boss approves testing them, the batch can proceed with that known risk. If not, convert #11/#13 into access/process timelines instead of body-outcome timelines.

4. **Text-wall scripts get a category-anchor exception, but not a transition exception.** The full stack can carry category context, but the first body card still has to answer the selected hook.

5. **Top-spender learning is now explicit in more than 30% of concepts.** Five concepts were updated to borrow more directly from Eligibility Payment Logos / the blue woman static, while the broader batch already carries the $8/day, $58/week, $0 down, no-insurance, and payment-plan signals.
