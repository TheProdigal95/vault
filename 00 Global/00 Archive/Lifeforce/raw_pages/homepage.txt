Become a Member

STEP 1

Measure your baseline.

We send a licensed phlebotomist to your home to conduct a blood draw measuring 50+ biomarkers. You’ll integrate any wearables you use, and complete an onboarding questionnaire, covering your history, lifestyle, and goals for the future.

STEP 2

Interpret your results.

We apply a proprietary, evidence-based algorithm to calculate your Lifescore — a single metric clarifying the overall state of your health. We also measure your quality of life, risk of dying early, and speed of aging. A Lifeforce clinician then further analyzes your data to identify your top priorities.

STEP 3

1:1 Clinician consult.

Spend 45 minutes on a telehealth consultation with a board-certified longevity medicine clinician to align on your priorities and review your personalized plan, including any supplements, hormones, peptides, pharmaceuticals, and lifestyle strategies appropriate for reaching your goals. Then, you’ll be matched with a health coach dedicated to getting you the best results.

STEP 4

Retest and refine.

Every three months we will conduct another at-home blood draw and telehealth consult to track progress, refine your program, and focus on your next set of goals.

Our longevity medicine model was developed with advisors from Harvard Medical School, Boston University, USC, and Brigham and Women’s Hospital.
