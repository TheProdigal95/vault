## Raw Notes — Batch Strategy

**Three conversion components confirmed by T004 data:** TRT angle + Identity Loss frame + Tony Robbins authority. Every concept in this batch should hit at least two of the three.

**TRT is the dominant signal.** Ad #2 (TRT 90-Day Timeline) = best CPP in account at $561. Ad #17 (Don't Skip this Step) = ROAS 9.30. 10 of 15 scripts are TRT-focused.

**Tony deployment — 7 formats available this batch:**
- Full A-roll narrator (Tony carries hook, body, close)
- Authority cutaway insert (5-10 sec inside protagonist/AI UGC story — proven at ROAS 9.30)
- Tweet reply → AI UGC pivot (Tony A-roll on crafted TRT tweet, hands off to AI creator for narrative control)
- Tony as hook, AI UGC as body/close (Tony opens with one line, creator runs the rest)
- B&A narration (Tony's "225 to 700" VO over B&W/color split — must show his face, not just voice)
- Timeline soundbite (Tony drops in at the mechanism beat — face shown)
- Authority close (protagonist/AI UGC full video, Tony appears last 5 sec as credibility stamp)
- Name-drop without A-roll (Tony cited in VO or caption — borrows authority without the clip)

**B&A format:** Use in two ways — (1) Tony B&A narration where Tony's HRT case study VO drives the split-screen, (2) B&A embedded as a visual component inside a testimonial narrative.

**Identity loss angle:** "Feeling like yourself again" is the emotional core of Ad #2's static success. Men in this audience describe testosterone loss as losing their identity — high school as the golden era, "I don't feel like myself." This language is the copy territory, not just the outcome.

**"Don't Skip this Step" insight:** Ad #17 vs #16 = same creative, different hook. Loss-aversion ("don't skip") at ROAS 9.30 vs curiosity ("if your energy sucks") at ROAS 0.29. Loss-aversion hook pattern is the winning structure.

**Benefits timeline for TRT:** Ad #2's $561 CPP proves the "clear process" static works. Video version adds temporal discounting in the first milestone. Honest window from first dose: mood/drive can shift within 3-7 days (genuinely hypogonadal men), energy/libido more like weeks 2-3, full body composition 6-7 weeks (Tony cites this directly in Clip 3, 05:15-05:27). Temporal discounting should land on mood/drive early, not physical transformation. Even in timeline format, include a Tony soundbite at the mechanism beat.

**High CTR / no purchase pattern:** Ads #5, #6, #7, #11, #13 all 3-7.5% CTR with 0 purchases. These are landing page or funnel issues, not creative failures. Don't kill those angles — they stop scrolls.

**Tony A-roll workflow — mandatory for all agents writing Tony concepts:** Full word-for-word transcripts with timestamps are in `Lifeforce/00 Assets/A-Roll - Tony Robbins.md`. Three clips available:
- **Clip 1 — Biomarkers** (1:30): nutrient/hormone testing basics, "flying blind" line (00:53-01:03), "new blood markers" (01:17-01:25)
- **Clip 2 — Hormones** (1:34): hormone optimization vs replacement, "body is not broken, but it's not maximizing" (00:32-00:36), 50+ hormones (00:36-00:41), low energy/brain fog/motivation list (00:49-00:57)
- **Clip 3 — Hormone Optimization Myths** (6:45): 1-2% testosterone loss after 35 (01:46-01:57), "loss of drive/energy/stubborn fat" (01:57-02:04), 170/175 case study (03:29-03:41), "500-600-700 range" (03:36-03:41), "6-7 weeks" transformation (05:15-05:27)

Agents must pull specific timestamps from this file when writing any concept that uses Tony A-roll. No fabricating Tony lines. If the script calls for a Tony soundbite, cite the clip name and timestamp range in the Visual column.

## Script Criteria

> **Baseline:** All rules in `00 Global/Criteria/` apply automatically.

### Lifeforce-Specific Rules

- **The "Broken" Reframe:** Never say the body is "broken." Use Tony's language: "Your body isn't broken, but it's not maximizing."
- **Reference Ranges:** Always reference the gap between "Normal" (264 ng/dL) and "Optimal" (600-900+). The gap is the selling point.
- **Price Framing:** Use "$49 to start" as the primary anchor. Transition to membership ($149/mo) only after clinical value is established.
- **No Injections in Visuals:** Mandatory compliance. Use at-home blood draw kits, telehealth screens, or lifestyle b-roll instead.
- **Tony A-roll rules:** Any time Tony's VO is used, his face must be shown — don't run his voice over unrelated B-roll. All Tony lines must be pulled from `Lifeforce/00 Assets/A-Roll - Tony Robbins.md` with the clip name and timestamp cited in the Visual column. No fabricated or paraphrased Tony quotes.
- **Tony + AI VO tagging (mixed formats):** When a script mixes Tony A-roll with AI/brand voiceover, the Voiceover column must use `[TONY]` as a prefix for every Tony A-roll line and `[AI narrator]` as a prefix for every brand/AI VO line. No unlabeled lines when both voices appear in the same script. In the References section, cite the specific clip (e.g., "TR-Clip 3") + Google Drive link + exact timestamp range for each Tony beat (e.g., "Pull 01:46-01:57 for the 1-2%/year decline line"). This applies to all 7 Tony deployment formats — any format where Tony A-roll and AI VO share the same script.
- **Temporal discounting for TRT:** Honest window from first dose — mood/drive: 3-7 days; energy/libido: weeks 2-3; full body composition: 6-7 weeks (Tony Clip 3, 05:15-05:27). Early milestones (mood, drive, mental clarity) are fair game for temporal discounting. Physical transformation claims belong at the 6-7 week mark or later. Do not claim physical changes in the first 1-2 weeks.
- **Loss-aversion hook pattern:** When using the "Don't Skip" / "I missed this" hook structure, the loss must be personal and specific — not generic fatigue. Tie it to identity ("drive," "presence," "feeling like yourself") not just symptoms.
- **Identity loss language:** Pull VoC language from TRT Reddit research. Men describe this as identity loss, not health decline. "I don't feel like myself" and high school / 20s as the reference point are the right territory.
- **Tweet format compliance:** Crafted tweets must read as real user tweets — no brand language, no CTAs in the tweet itself. The tweet is the hook; Tony and the AI UGC creator handle the narrative.
