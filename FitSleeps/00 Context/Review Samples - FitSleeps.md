# Review Samples - FitSleeps

> **Source:** ~600 Trustpilot reviews (4.3/5 average), supplemented by RatingFacts and site testimonials
> **Generated:** 2026-04-02
> **Purpose:** Raw material for ad copywriters -- verbatim reviews organized by persona/use-case and sentiment. All quotes are exact wording from reviewers. Pull directly from these for ad scripts, hooks, and social proof.

---

## How to Use This Document

1. **For ad hooks/headlines:** Scan the category that matches your target persona. The strongest lines are the ones that name a specific pain point AND a specific outcome.
2. **For objection handling:** Categories 8-13 contain the real negative reviews. Understanding what people complain about lets you preempt objections in copy.
3. **For story-driven ads:** Look for reviews that contain a before/after arc -- the reviewer describes life before FitSleeps, then life after. These are your narrative ad scripts.
4. **Some reviews appear in multiple categories** because they serve multiple persona angles. This is intentional -- a review about a teen with ADHD belongs in both "Heavy Sleepers" and "Parents Buying for Teens."
