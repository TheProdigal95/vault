## Raw Notes — Batch Strategy

> **Core strategy:** Broaden the Lifeforce video portfolio by bridging high-CTR native hooks (Parenting) with high-ROAS logical frameworks (Comparison/Crossout). Testing Peptides as a sub-angle in 3 concepts.
>
> **Data-backed pivots (Agree points from May 15 Audit):** 
> - **The Parenting Bridge:** High CTRs (2.05%) on "Raising my Daughter" and "As a Mom" static ads show that parental duty is the strongest scroll-stopper. We are porting this into male TRT/Hormone scripts (The "Drained Dad" angle). **Visuals:** POV Cinematic montage of family life (park, beach, morning chaos) contrasted with 60% capacity fatigue cues.
> - **Tony Access 2.0:** Moving away from "Sale" energy (0.36 ROAS) toward "Billionaire Access" (0.67 ROAS). **Tone:** Intimate "Behind-the-scenes" interview style rather than the loud stage persona. Frame Lifeforce as the bridge to what high-performers do that standard labs miss.
> - **The $1.37 Anchor:** Integrating price-per-day logic into comparison ads to justify the membership layer (based on the 0.38 ROAS success of the $1.37 static).
> - **Crossout Logic:** Doubling down on the 0.92 ROAS "Stop Guessing" winner.
> - **Peptide Sub-Test:** Capping at 3 concepts (Willpower, Sermorelin, Nutrition Reframe).
> - **Tony Integration:** Using Tony as the "Bridging Authority" across 5+ concepts to maximize trust signal.
>
> **May 18 Full Analysis Updates:**
> - **Crossout is the most under-tested winner:** 0.92 ROAS at only $1.4K spend — deserves 5-10x budget immediately. Account's single best-performing creative format. Priority #1 for production.
> - **Male UGC gap confirmed:** Zero male UGC testimonials exist in the entire library. Sara's "14 markers vs 50+" structure is the blueprint — adapted for TRT men. Replaced Concept 14 (Estrogen Cliff).
> - **Women's funnel mismatch:** Women's creative has strong CTRs (We're Sorry 1.93%, Raising My Daughter 2.06%) but 0 purchases across all women's ads. LP is TRT/male-optimized. Concept 4 (Tony women's health) kept — Tony's authority angle is distinct. Verify women's LP before scaling any women's concepts.
> - **Tony quote quality rule:** Emotional quotes convert (QuoteDream "a million dreams" = 0.67 ROAS, $1,448 AOV). Brand-speak is dead on arrival (QuoteGoal "affordable and accessible" = 0.00 ROAS). Applied to Concept 2.

## Script Criteria

> **Baseline:** All rules in `00 Global/Criteria/` apply automatically. 

### Lifeforce-Specific Rules (Peptides Batch)

- **The "Broken" Reframe:** Never say the body is "broken." Use Tony's language: "Your body isn't broken, but it's not maximizing."
- **Peptides Shorthand:** Use "Peptides" in hooks for native/insider feel, but provide specific context (Sermorelin / GLP-1) within the first 10 seconds.
- **Reference Ranges:** Always reference the gap between "Normal" (264 ng/dL) and "Optimal" (600-900+). The gap is the selling point.
- **Price Framing:** Use "$49 to start" as the primary anchor for the diagnostic layer. Transition to membership ($149/mo) only after the clinical value is established.
- **No Injections in Visuals:** Mandatory compliance rule. Use at-home blood draw kits, telehealth screens, or lifestyle b-roll instead.
