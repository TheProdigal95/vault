Can I take IM8 while on Ozempic, Wegovy, or Mounjaro?
+

Yes. IM8 is a food-based nutritional supplement, not a medication. It contains vitamins, minerals, superfoods, and probiotics — all of which are safe to take alongside GLP-1 medications. However, we always recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement, especially if you're on prescription medication.

Will IM8 interfere with my GLP-1 medication?
+

No. IM8 is designed to complement your medication by filling the nutritional gaps that reduced food intake creates. There are no known interactions between IM8's ingredients and GLP-1 receptor agonists. The nutrients in IM8 are the same ones your doctor would recommend you get from food — just in a more concentrated, bioavailable form.

When should I take IM8 if I'm on GLP-1 medication?
+

We recommend taking IM8 in the morning with or after a light meal. Since GLP-1 medications can cause nausea, taking IM8 with food helps ensure comfortable absorption. Many users mix it into a morning smoothie for the best experience.

How is IM8 different from a regular multivitamin?
+

A standard multivitamin contains 20-30 nutrients at minimal doses. IM8 delivers 90+ ingredients including superfoods, adaptogens, prebiotics, probiotics, postbiotics, essential amino acids, and CoQ10 — all in forms your body can actually absorb. It's NSF Certified for Sport, meaning every batch is independently tested for purity and potency.

Will IM8 help with the hair loss I'm experiencing?
+

Hair thinning on GLP-1 medications is typically caused by nutrient deficiencies — particularly biotin, zinc, iron, and protein. IM8 provides clinical doses of all these nutrients. While individual results vary, many users report improvements in hair, skin, and nail health within 60-90 days of consistent use.

What does IM8 taste like?
+

IM8 has a light, refreshing açaí berry flavor that mixes easily with water. Unlike many greens powders, it doesn't have a strong earthy or grassy taste. Most users find it pleasant to drink, and it works great blended into smoothies as well.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. We offer a money-back guarantee on every plan — up to 90 days on the 90-day plan and 30 days on the 30-day plan. If you don't feel the difference after trying IM8, contact our customer service team for a full refund — no questions asked.
