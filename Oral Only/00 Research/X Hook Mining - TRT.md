## Search: `"TRT" "weekly shot" "crash" OR "rollercoaster" testosterone`

**What came back:**
- Weekly TRT shots are repeatedly framed as a peak/trough problem: “spike then crater,” “rollercoaster,” “crash.”
- Community solutions usually involve splitting doses, SubQ, EOD, or daily microdosing — useful because it proves men are actively trying to fix delivery curve problems.
- For Oral Only, use this as **standard TRT friction / delivery curve curiosity**, not as a claim that Oral Only creates stable levels.
