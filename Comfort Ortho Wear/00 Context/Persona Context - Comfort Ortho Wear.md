## The Long-Shift Standing Worker — "My feet are destroying my career"

**Audience share:** 22.4% of reviews (149 reviews). Primary persona. Precision: 68% (directional).
**Top 3 pains:** Foot pain that makes shifts unbearable, inability to finish 12-hour shifts without agony, dreading going back to work because of shoe failure

### Part 1 — How ComfortWear Connects

This persona's core problem is professional: their feet hurt badly enough that their job becomes a daily ordeal. They are already wearing "work shoes" — but those shoes are failing them on 12-hour floors. ComfortWear addresses this directly through two mechanisms. First, the slip-resistant outsole meets a practical safety requirement that generic cushioned sneakers don't satisfy — hospital, kitchen, and retail floors demand it. Second, the combination of ORTHO-WEAR Heel-Hugging Tech and ULTIMATE INSOLES provides multi-hour cushioning that conventional foam-soled shoes lose by hour four. For this persona, "nurse-recommended" framing is not marketing — it's a peer endorsement from someone who has the same shift structure they do. The price point ($59.95–$79.95) is also decisive: Dansko and OrthoFeet cost $100–175 and reviewers in this persona explicitly compare on value.
