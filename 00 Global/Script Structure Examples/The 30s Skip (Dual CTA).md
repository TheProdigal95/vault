# Script Structure: The 30s Skip (Dual CTA)

The **30s Skip** (or Dual CTA) architecture is designed for skippable ad formats (like AppLovin or YouTube). It ensures the primary hook, product reveal, and a soft "CTA #1" land within the first 30 seconds, while reserving deeper proof points for the "Part 2" viewers who choose to stay.

### **Core Breakdown**
- **Dual Architecture:** 
    - **Part 1 (0:00-0:30):** Hook + Mechanism + Product Reveal + CTA #1.
    - **Part 2 (0:30-0:50+):** Expanded Proof + Authority + Final CTA #2.
- **Product Reveal:** Must name the product in Part 1 before the skip window.
- **Pacing:** Brisk and energetic in Part 1 to hit the 30s mark. Slightly more measured in Part 2.
- **Key Requirement:** A hard cut or strong bridge at the CTA #1 transition (around 0:25-0:28).
