## Script #4 — Best Friend on the Floor (Social Proof Narrative)

**Persona:** Long-Shift Standing Worker
**Structure:** First-person narrative (UGC-style)
**Duration:** ~67 seconds
**Caption style:** Bold sans-serif
**Close type:** Story close
**VO voice:** Female, 35-45, warm and casual. Voice-memo energy — telling a friend, not performing.

### Concept

A nurse tells the story of how her best friend on the floor kept bugging her to try these shoes. She was skeptical because she'd already wasted money on Skechers and expensive orthotics. But after borrowing her friend's pair for one shift, she ordered her own that night. The narrative arc is dismissed (skepticism) → discovered (borrowed the shoes) → results (stopped limping, three other nurses ordered). Social proof review cards pop up at three points mid-video as overlays reinforcing the peer recommendation. Warm, authentic, conversational — sounds like a voice memo, not a script.
