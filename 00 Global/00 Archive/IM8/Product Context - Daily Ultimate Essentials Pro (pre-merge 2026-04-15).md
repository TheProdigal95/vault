- "Replaces 16+ supplements" (simplification)
- "NSF Certified for Sport" (trust)
- "The New Gold Standard" (aspirational positioning)
- "Co-founded," not "endorsed by" (Beckham's structural role)
- "Stevia-free" (AG1 attack vector)

- "CRT8," "Cell Rejuvenation Technology" (proprietary IP)
- "Mayo Clinic," "NASA," "Yale," "Cedars-Sinai" (institutional names)

### Words/Phrases to AVOID
- "Cures" or "treats" any disease
- "Better than AG1" (show, don't tell — let the comparison speak)
- Specific disease prevention claims
- Unsubstantiated weight loss numbers
- "Cheap" or "affordable" (brand is premium, not value)
- "Guaranteed results" without qualification
