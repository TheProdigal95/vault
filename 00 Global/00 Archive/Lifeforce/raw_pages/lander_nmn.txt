Invest In Your Biological Future

NAD+, or nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, is a powerful molecule that regulates and energizes our cells. Problem is, we make less and less as we age. In fact, we lose half of our NAD+ reserve by the time we hit midlife — resulting in obesity, chronic fatigue, diminished brain function, and accelerated aging. That’s where supplementing with NMN, or nicotinamide mononucleotide, comes in. Once inside the body, NMN effectively triggers the production of NAD+.
