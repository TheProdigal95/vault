## Sleep Reset

**Stats:** App Store 4.8★ (3,300+ ratings). CBT-I + behavioral sleep coaching app with human coach access via text. Program runs 8 weeks. Pricing: $297 promotional / $497 list (as of 2023–2024) for the full program; also offers subscription billing in some configurations (confirmed by Trustpilot reviewer paying $1,500 total). FSA/HSA eligible. Also offers clinician visit add-ons and at-home sleep apnea testing. Available on iOS and Android. US-based coaches, English only.

**Fatal Flaw:** Insurance Bait-and-Switch — app shows insurance as accepted during onboarding, then fails to deliver reimbursability, leaving patients with hundreds to thousands of dollars in unexpected out-of-pocket costs with no superbill and no meaningful refund.

---

### Theme A: Deceptive Insurance & Billing Practices

> "I enrolled in Sleep Reset following a referral from my primary physician. During onboarding, I selected my insurance provider, and the app indicated that my insurance was accepted. This created a clear expectation that services would be eligible for reimbursement, and I relied on that representation when deciding to proceed... In total, I paid $1,500 to Sleep Reset under the false expectation that services would be reimbursable. Despite the financial impact, the only resolution offered was a $75 refund, which is comparable to burning someone's house down and offering an Amazon gift card to rebuild it." — Jake, 1★, Trustpilot, Sep 2025

> "After sharing my experience online, I received a flood of messages from numerous former patients who described similar situations. There were various accounts of users being told their insurance was accepted during onboarding, only to later learn it was not. Others described being charged earlier than expected during free trials, subscriptions continuing beyond the advertised program length without clear notice, or discovering only during cancellation attempts that there were lower-cost plan options that had not been presented upfront." — Jake, 1★, Trustpilot, Sep 2025

> "All of this paints a grim picture of a healthcare provider financially preying on sleep-deprived patients with deceptive marketing and billing practices." — Jake, 1★, Trustpilot, Sep 2025

**Stellar Sleep positioning angle:** Stellar Sleep does not misrepresent insurance coverage or hide pricing tiers. Straightforward subscription — what you see is what you pay.

## Stellar Sleep's Own Competitive Positioning (Self-Derived from Reviews + Site)

In the absence of validated direct competitors with scraped reviews, the following competitive angles derive from how Stellar Sleep's own satisfied users describe the app's advantage over the landscape they came from.

### Category: =="Not another meditation app"==
The most common competitive frame in positive reviews is contrast with generic relaxation apps:
- "I like the emphasis on building ==skills that I can use to tackle my specific sleep issues vs other apps I've tried which try to just calm you down with generic seeming meditation==." — `appstore-us-most-9687450171`
- "It's not just meditation — it addresses root causes."

**Stellar Sleep positioning angle:** The "CBT-I vs. meditation" wedge is already embedded in customer language. This is a natural creative frame.

### Category: "==Not just advice you could find on Google=="
Several 1★ reviewers say the content is generic (suggesting a portion of the base expects more). But the 5★ base explicitly disagrees:
- "The knowledge and practical education will last a lifetime. There is a ton of misconceptions about sleep and this app addresses many of them." — `appstore-us-most-11061664330`
- "This app is different because of the education piece." — `appstore-us-most-11123687667`

**Stellar Sleep positioning angle:** The education component — specifically the "why" behind CBT-I techniques — is what converts skeptics. This is the key differentiator from free online resources.

### Category: "Not a pill — and better than a pill long-term"
- "3 months later I'm off my sleeping pills and no longer have crushing anxiety about going to bed." — `appstore-nz-most-11472026575`
- "By the middle of the second week I stopped taking melatonin and about 10 other supplements." — `appstore-us-most-11123687667`

**Stellar Sleep positioning angle:** Medication exit is a verifiable, named outcome in reviews. This is a uniquely strong positioning claim — pills treat symptoms, CBT-I eliminates them.
